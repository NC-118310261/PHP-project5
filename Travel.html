London

I travelled to London with my mom during my Christmas holidays in 2018.
We stayed four nights in London City.
It is a lovely place.
We visited the shops on Oxford Street and Regent Street.
We also took a trip to Buckingham Palace to see where the Queen of England lives.
It is a beautiful building.
We also visited to famous shop Harrods.
It is a very big shop.
We went to see the West End show, Les Miserables, in The Queens Theatre.
It was an amazing performance.
